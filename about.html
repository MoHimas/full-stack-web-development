About Malcolm

Introduction
Welcome to Malcolm Lismore Photography! Discover the art and passion behind the lens as Malcolm captures the natural beauty of Scotland and beyond.

Biography
Malcolm Lismore, a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland, has dedicated his life to capturing the essence of the natural world. With years of experience and a deep love for the outdoors, Malcolm's work reflects his unique perspective and artistic vision.

Photoghraphy Passion
Malcolm's biggest passion lies in photographing the rugged Scottish landscapes, its diverse wildlife, and coastal birds. His portfolio showcases the breathtaking beauty of these scenes, revealing the intricate details and stunning vistas that make Scotland unique. Beyond landscapes and wildlife, Malcolm's versatility shines through in his work with weddings, portraits, and special events, where he skillfully captures memorable moments.

services Offered
Malcolm Lismore Photography provides a range of professional services, including:
Wedding Photography: Capturing the joy and emotion of your special day with a blend of candid and posed shots.
Portrait Sessions: Creating timeless portraits that reflect your personality and style.
Event Photography: Documenting special events with a keen eye for detail and storytelling.
Nature and Wildlife Photography: Offering prints and digital downloads of Malcolm's stunning nature and wildlife images.
